Guide for walking a dog

  • Walk in Front of Your Dog Walking in front of your dog allows you to be seen as the pack leader.
  • Use a Short Dog Leash This allows you to have more control.
  • Give Yourself Enough Time for the Dog Walk Dogs, like humans, are diurnal, so taking walks in the morning is ideal
